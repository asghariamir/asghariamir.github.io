Interactive Learning Apps

A collection of web applications designed for teaching and exploring concepts in mathematics.

Fractals and Chaos

Cantor Set Explorer

Generate stages of the Cantor set, explore its properties and calculate its box-counting dimension.

Crumpled Paper Dimension

An interactive lab to analyze the fractal dimension of a crumpled ball by plotting its mass vs. radius on a log-log scale.

Logistic Map Explorer

Visualize the behavior of the logistic map through its bifurcation diagram and orbit plots. Estimate the fractal dimension of its strange attractor.

Sierpiński Triangle Explorer

Construct the Sierpiński triangle recursively. Includes interactive lab for calculating the box-counting dimension.

Arithmetics and Algebra

Multiplicative Sums

An interactive color pad for exploring the multiplicative structure of numbers, particularly the sums of the first N powers.
