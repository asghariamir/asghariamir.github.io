🃏 Trig 'Families'

A maths card game inspired by the classic Happy Families. Collect complete sets of 4 cards by asking other players for the cards you need!

The Four Card Types

Each family has exactly 4 cards — but the card types are not labelled. You must read the mathematics to work out what each card shows!

Function · Graph · Value · Unit Circle — can you tell which is which?

Colour Coding

Some values look the same across families (e.g. sin(30°) and cos(60°) both equal ½). The card colour tells you which family it belongs to!

📖 How to Play

🃏

Deal

All cards are dealt evenly to the players.

🔍

Identify

Study your cards — figure out what type each one is from its content.

👆

Click to Ask

Click a missing card slot in your hand, choose the type you think it is, and ask a player for it.

🏆

Win

Collect the most completed sets of four!

Important Rules

  1. You must already hold at least one card from a family before asking for another.
  2. Click a missing card slot (dashed outline) to begin your ask.
  3. Choose from all four card types — but be careful! If you ask for a type you already hold, you lose your turn.
  4. Completed sets remain visible as trophies in your hand.
