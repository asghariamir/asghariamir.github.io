📖 Rules — Trig 'Families'

Inspired by Happy Families, designed by M.Reachable.

How to Play

  1. Study your cards — figure out each card's function, angle, and type.
  2. On your turn, fill in the sentence: Ask [player]: "Do you have the [type] for [func]([angle])?"
  3. If the asked player has that card, it goes to your Received tray. Tap to add it to your hand. You go again!
  4. If not — your turn ends.
  5. Ask for the wrong thing and you might get a card that doesn't help!
  6. Complete sets of 4 to score. Most sets wins!
